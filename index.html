About Gary Law

Decades of Dedicated Public Service

Gary Law has spent his entire professional life defending public safety and advocating for community collaboration. As a former RCMP Senior Officer, Gary understands that community security is not just about police visibility; it is about cross-cultural respect, active listening, and practical strategic foresight.

For over 30 years, Richmond has been the community where Gary chose to plant deep roots, raise his family, and dedicate his volunteer leadership. Over these decades, he has witnessed Richmond grow from a suburban city to an energetic, multi-cultural municipal hub. Gary wants to ensure that as we grow, we retain the safety, clean neighborhoods, and robust civic pride that make Richmond beautiful.

Professional Civic Timeline

  • Senior RCMP Officer Career Managed municipal safety strategies, directed incident responses, and initiated regional safety clinics.
  • Awarded RCMP Police Community Relations Award Honored for high-level success in fostering dialogue and building bridges across cultural community divisions.
  • Youth Leadership & Mentorship Initiatives Initiated peer-mentoring partnerships guiding local Richmond youth on leadership and civic accountability.

Campaign Knowledge Hub

All Questions & Answers

Who is Gary Law?

Gary Law is a former RCMP Senior Officer, a Richmond resident of over 30 years, and a dedicated community volunteer. He is the recipient of multiple RCMP Police Community Relations Awards and has led numerous local youth mentoring programs.

Why is Gary Law running for Richmond City Council?

Gary is running to apply his professional security, community policing background, and deep community insights directly to our local leadership framework. He believes City Council needs clear public accountability and expert oversight on safety infrastructure.

What are Gary Law's priorities?

His core priorities are: Community Safety, Traffic & Transportation, Transparency & Accountability, Seniors & Families, and maintaining a Livable Richmond.

What is Gary Law's approach to community safety?

Gary leverages his experience as a former RCMP officer to support proactive safety measures. This includes expanding neighborhood block watch initiatives, increasing police patrol visibility in key spaces, and coordinating mental health professionals alongside emergency services.

How can I support the campaign?

You can help by joining as a volunteer, hosting a lawn sign, spreading the word, or sharing your ideas with Gary. Click "Volunteer" on our connect panel to get registered.
